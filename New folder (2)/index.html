About Avifric

Your Trusted
Aviation Partner
in Africa

Avifric Limited is a Kenya-based aviation ground handling and support services company headquartered at JKIA Nairobi. We specialize in aircraft handling, landing permits, crew logistics, fuelling coordination, and aviation support services across Africa.

With deep industry knowledge and strategic partnerships across the continent, Avifric is positioned as the leading aviation support partner for international operators entering Africa.

Our Mission
To deliver reliable, efficient and professional aviation ground handling that empowers airlines and operators to navigate Africa with confidence.
02
Our Vision
To be the most trusted and comprehensive aviation support partner across the African continent, enabling seamless air operations.
03
Our Advantage
Pan-Africa permit networks, 24/7 operations, regulatory compliance, and an experienced team at one of Africa's busiest international airports.
